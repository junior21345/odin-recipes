Spicy Pork

Delicious spicy pork

Recipe great for the family

Ingredients

    2 tablespoons hot chili paste(you can use sambal oelek, but sriracha can also work)

    2 tablespoons soy sauce

    2 tablespoons brown sugar

    11-inch piece of ginger, peeled and finely grated

    1 garlic clove, finely grated

    1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin, very thinly sliced

Steps

    1. Shake all the sauce ingredients in a jar. Pour the sauce over the pork and allow it to rest for 20minutes - 1 hour.

    2. Heat a good searing skillet(such as cast iron) over high heat. Add just a little bit of vcegetable oil.

    3. When the oil is hot and shiny, add the pork in a single layer(you may need to do this in batches). Cook, undisturbed, for 1-2 minutes until you are getting that nice cararmelized look. Flip and repeat to finish.

    4. That's it! Enjoy your flavortown masterpiece.
